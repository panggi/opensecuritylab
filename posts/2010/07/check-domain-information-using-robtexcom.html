Check domain information using robtex.com

July 23 2010, 11:19 PM  by Panggi Libersa Jasri Akadol

Simple , just go to http://robtex.com and then insert the domain name at the top left of the website , click "Search".

After some results appear , click at the link addressed like this : http://www.robtex.com/dns/www.domain.tld.html

there are 8 tabs containing various information about the domain :

Summary

Records

Graphs

Shared

Whois

Blacklists

Analysis

Contact

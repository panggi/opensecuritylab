3rd Cloud Computing World Forum - Asia

October 26 2010, 2:57 AM  by Panggi Libersa Jasri Akadol

Taking place on the 17th and 18th November 2010 the Free-to-attend event will feature all of the key players within the Cloud Computing and SaaS market providing an introduction, discussion and look into the future for the ICT industry.

The Cloud Computing World Forum Asia is the place to meet all the key decision makers from all of the cloud service and SaaS providers in one place. The conference will address the current state of the market, the business value of cloud and SaaS, deployment strategies and integration techniques, security and privacy issues and will take a look into what the future holds for the Cloud Computing and SaaS industry.

The show will include a free-to-attend exhibition with scenario based workshop area and key service providers demonstrating their products.

The event will provide great networking opportunities with industry leaders, presenting you with the opportunity to meet and do business with likeminded individuals.

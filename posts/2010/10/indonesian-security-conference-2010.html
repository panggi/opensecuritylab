Indonesian Security Conference 2010

October 4 2010, 12:18 AM  by Panggi Libersa Jasri Akadol

Indonesian Security Conference 2010, better known IDSECCONF2010 is the annual event held in cooperation organization / non-profit IT community in cooperation with the Ministry of Communications and Information Affairs, and other related parties that aims to enlighten the general public, students, and among IT Security professionals, especially regarding the various issues of current information technology security.

This annual event has been held since 2008 in Yogyakarta Special Region in cooperation with the UPN Veteran Yogyakarta, then resumed in 2009 at the Jakarta regional in cooperation with Al-Azhar University of Indonesia. And in 2010 it will be held in Bali, in cooperation and housed in STIKOM Bali on Saturday and Sunday, 16-17 October 2010.

IDSECCONF2010 theme is "Security in electronic banking and electronic payment transaction", will be carried on for two days, with 4 keynote speeches, 8 speakers, 2 panel discussions and game hacking.
